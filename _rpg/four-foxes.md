## Overview

The Four Foxes is an investigative diceless RPG set in 1926. Fast-paced gameplay with characters full of personality, featuring lots of opportunities for engaging roleplay and bickering between four very distinctive characters.

## Key Features

- **Diceless System**: Focus on narrative and character interaction rather than random chance
- **Pre-Generated Characters**: Four distinct, fully-realized characters with rich personalities
- **Investigation Focus**: Designed around mystery-solving and detective work
- **Fast-Paced**: Streamlined rules for quick, engaging sessions
- **Flexible Cast**: Available with both brothers and sisters versions

## What Makes It Special

- Characters have built-in relationships and conflicts that drive roleplay
- Mechanics encourage cooperation while maintaining character distinctiveness
- Perfect for one-shots or short campaigns
- Easy to learn for newcomers to RPGs
- Rich 1920s historical setting with authentic period details

## Game Master Notes

- Scenarios should focus on investigation and mystery
- Character conflicts are features, not bugs - embrace the drama
- The diceless system puts emphasis on creative problem-solving
- Works excellently for convention games or introducing new players
